## üéØ Function
The generator simplifies the task of having to think of a strong password. How?

I used a simple intuitive and single screen, as well as its features, which are:

### 1. Copy the generate password.
The copy button have the fuction of copy the generate password.

### 2. Set the amount of characters that the password will have.
This option allows you to set the characters that the password will have, the minimum is four and the maximum is twenty-two.

### 3. Insert at least one lowercase letter in the password that will be generate.
The checkbox with the option to insert lowercase makes that the password have at least one lowercase letter, among them are abcdefghijklmnopqrstuvwxyz.

### 4. Insert at least one uppercase letter in the password that will be generate.
The checkbox with the option to insert uppercase makes that the password have at least one uppercase letter, among them are ABCDEFGHIJKLMNOPQRSTUVWXYZ.

### 5. Insert at least one number in the password that will be generate.
The checkbox with the option to insert a number makes that the password have at least one number, among them are 0123456789.

### 6. Insert at least one special letter in the password that will be generate.
The checkbox with the option to insert special character makes the password have at least one special character, among them are !@#$%&*()_+-*/=,.<>;:.

### 7. Generate the password according to your preferences.
The generate button have the fuction of generate the password according to your preferences.
